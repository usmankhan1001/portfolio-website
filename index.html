Hi, I'm Usman Khan

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), one of the top engineering institutions in Pakistan. During my studies, I developed a strong foundation in both theoretical and applied mechanics, with a growing passion for designing intelligent and efficient mechanical systems.

A significant part of my academic and project work revolves around Finite Element Analysis (FEA), particularly static structural simulations to assess component strength and safety under expected loads. I also perform modal analysis to evaluate vibrational characteristics and avoid critical resonance in sensitive mechanical assemblies.

I specialize in 3D CAD modeling using tools like SolidWorks and Fusion 360, turning design ideas into detailed engineering models. These models are not just digital artifacts — I bring them to life using hands-on manufacturing techniques, ensuring the final product performs as intended in the real world.

Currently, I’m transitioning into the field of robotics and artificial intelligence, integrating mechanical design with smart technologies. My vision is to contribute to systems that are both functionally robust and intelligently responsive — solutions that have practical value and a lasting impact.
